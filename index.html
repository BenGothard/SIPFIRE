The SIPFIRE Map

Master the Seven Disciplines

Balance your energy across every pillar to create a life that is both meaningful and unstoppable.

Spiritual

Align with purpose. Practice gratitude, presence, and values-driven decisions so every action radiates meaning.

  • Daily reflection or meditation
  • Document one lesson learned
  • Act in alignment with your core values

Intellectual

Feed your curiosity. Study, learn, and experiment to sharpen your mind and decision-making power.

  • Read or listen to 10 pages/minutes
  • Capture one insight in your SIPFIRE journal
  • Teach what you learned to someone else

Physical

Honor your body. Move daily, fuel intentionally, and recover to stay ready for every mission.

  • Complete a purposeful workout
  • Hydrate early and often
  • Track sleep quality and recovery

Financial

Command your resources. Budget, build assets, and invest in future freedom.

  • Review cash flow and spending
  • Automate a wealth-building move
  • Support aligned projects & creators

Industrial

Create with purpose. Launch ideas, build systems, and ship work that compounds impact.

  • Ship one deliverable or iteration
  • Automate or streamline a workflow
  • Document your operating system

Relational

Invest in people. Cultivate deep connections and show up for those who matter.

  • Send gratitude to someone in your circle
  • Schedule quality time with intention
  • Listen with curiosity before speaking

Emotional

Lead yourself. Master your state, process emotions, and transform challenges into growth.

  • Check in with your emotional dashboard
  • Identify one belief to upgrade
  • Celebrate a win, no matter how small
Daily Rhythm

The SIPFIRE Compounder

Stack small, intentional moves for exponential impact. Track your streaks and measure your momentum.

Morning Ignite

Start the day aligned and energized.

  • 5 minutes of breath work or prayer
  • Set your top three targets
  • Hydrate and move for 10 minutes

Midday Momentum

Recalibrate and keep compounding.

  • Micro-learning session (podcast, course)
  • Ship a deliverable that moves the mission
  • Send gratitude or encouragement to a teammate

Evening Integration

Reflect, celebrate, and prepare for tomorrow.

  • Journal the day: wins, lessons, emotions
  • Review finances & plan tomorrow's power plays
  • Disconnect tech & protect deep rest
